Purple Team

In cybersecurity, the term "purple team" refers to a collaborative approach that combines elements of both the red team and blue team methodologies. The red team is responsible for simulating attacks and probing the system's vulnerabilities, while the blue team focuses on defending and mitigating those attacks. The purple team concept aims to bridge the gap between offensive and defensive security by fostering cooperation and knowledge sharing between these teams.
